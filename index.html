Biography

Welcome to my homepage! I am currently pursuing an MS degree in the School of Computer and Communication Sciences at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) and doing a master's project in the Laboratory of Sensing and Networking Systems under the supervision of Prof. Haitham Hassanieh. Previously, I was a research intern at Max Planck Institute for Informatics (MPI-INF), hosted by Dr. Yiting Xia. During that time, I also worked closely with Prof. Savvas Zannettou from Delft University of Technology (TU Delft). Before that, I worked as a research assistant in the Visual Intelligence for Transportation Laboratory under the supervision of Prof. Alexandre Alahi. I received my BE degree at Nanjing University of Science and Technology (NJUST), advised by Prof. Yijin Zhang. You can find me via email or WhatsApp.
