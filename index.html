Biography

Welcome to my homepage! I am currently pursuing an M.S. degree in the School of Computer and Communication Sciences at the École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne and doing a research internship at the Max Planck Institute for Informatics under the supervision of Dr. Yiting Xia. Meanwhile, I am also working closely with Prof. Savvas Zannettou from Delft University of Technology. Prior to that, I worked as a research assistant at the VITA lab under the supervision of Prof. Alexandre Alahi. I received my B.E. degree at Nanjing University of Science and Technology, advised by Prof. Yijin Zhang. You can find me via email or WhatsApp.
