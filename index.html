Biography

Welcome to my homepage! I am currently pursuing a PhD degree in the Laboratory of Sensing and Networking Systems at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) under the supervision of Prof. Haitham Hassanieh. I earned my MS degree from the School of Computer and Communication Sciences at EPFL. Previously, I worked as a research intern at Max Planck Institute for Informatics (MPI-INF), where I was hosted by Dr. Yiting Xia and collaborated closely with Prof. Savvas Zannettou from Delft University of Technology (TU Delft). Before that, I was a research assistant in the Visual Intelligence for Transportation Laboratory, supervised by Prof. Alexandre Alahi. I received my BE degree at Nanjing University of Science and Technology (NJUST), advised by Prof. Yijin Zhang. You can reach me via email or WhatsApp.
