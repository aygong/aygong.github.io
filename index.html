Biography

Welcome to my homepage! I am currently pursuing a PhD degree in the Laboratory of Sensing and Networking Systems at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL), under the supervision of Haitham Hassanieh, and collaborating closely with Dan Mihai Dumitriu from Pavonis LLC. I earned my MS degree from the School of Computer and Communication Sciences at EPFL. Previously, I was a research intern at Max Planck Institute for Informatics (MPI-INF), where I was hosted by Yiting Xia and worked closely with Savvas Zannettou from Delft University of Technology (TU Delft). Before that, I worked as a research assistant in the Visual Intelligence for Transportation Laboratory, supervised by Alexandre Alahi. I received my BE degree at Nanjing University of Science and Technology (NJUST), advised by Yijin Zhang. You can reach me via email or WhatsApp.
